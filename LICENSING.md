No further action is required beyond AGPL-3.0 compliance.

## 2. Commercial — NortheBridge Access License Agreement

If you want to use LuminalVGD in a product **without** releasing your source
code, the NortheBridge Foundation offers commercial terms under the
**NortheBridge Access License Agreement (NALA)**. A commercial license removes
the AGPL-3.0 source-disclosure obligations for the licensed product.

Contact the NortheBridge Foundation to arrange commercial licensing.

## Contributions

So that the Foundation can continue to offer both licenses, all contributions
require a signed Contributor License Agreement (CLA) granting NortheBridge the
right to distribute the contribution under the AGPL-3.0 **and** under the
NortheBridge Access License Agreement. Contributors retain copyright in their
work. Pull requests cannot be merged until the CLA is on file.
